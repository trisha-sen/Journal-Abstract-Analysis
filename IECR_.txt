This is a dataset containing abstract and author details of all papaers in the journal "Industrial & Engineering Chemistry Research" (https://pubs.acs.org/journal/iecred). The database was constructed by scraping the IECR website. Following are the features of the dataframe:

Category                33082 non-null object:
Category of the article as classified by the journal

Corresponding Author    32537 non-null object:
The last author in the author-list. Typically this is the advisor/professor who guides the research

#Authors                33083 non-null int64:
The total number of contributing authors reportes in the paper

Title                   33083 non-null object:
Title of the paper 

Date                    33083 non-null datetime64[ns]:
Date of first online publication

Journal                 33083 non-null object:
Name of the journal in which the paper is published

Volume                  33083 non-null int64:
Journal Volume #

Issue                   33083 non-null int64:
Journal Issue #

Abstract                33083 non-null object:
Paper abstract

DOI                     33083 non-null object:
DOI number of the paper

Authors                 33083 non-null object:
Full list of authors in the paper

Type                    33083 non-null object:
Whether the item is a research paper or some other commentary

Year                    33083 non-null int64:
Year in which the article was published
